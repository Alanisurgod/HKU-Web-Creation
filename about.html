About this companion site

The HKU Freshman Guide is a small, static website meant to improve orientation for typical first-year tasks. It is not an official HKU publication and does not speak for the University.

Ethics & transparency

  • We avoid fake notices and invented policy text—official pages are linked instead.
  • Wellbeing copy is intentionally cautious: suggestions, not diagnosis or treatment.
  • Academic integrity defers to your course and HKU’s authoritative documents.

AI assistance disclosure

On-site wording, information architecture, SVG illustrations, and front-end code were drafted with assistance from an AI coding tool under human direction. Facts that can drift (dates, URLs, policy names) should always be verified on official HKU pages, especially before assessments or formal decisions.

Improvements

This repository is designed for static hosting. If you maintain a fork, keep external links current and preserve the independence disclaimer in the footer.

Reminder: not affiliated with HKU communications unless you explicitly decide otherwise in your own deployment.
