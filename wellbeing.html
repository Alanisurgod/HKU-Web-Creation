Wellbeing: steady rhythms, real support

This page offers general self-care ideas for typical students. It is not medical advice. For persistent symptoms or crises, use professional services and emergency numbers appropriate to your location.

If you or someone else is at immediate risk, contact local emergency services first, then reach out through official HKU support channels.

Everyday patterns that help many people

  • Sleep as a schedule item rather than “what’s left” at midnight.
  • Daylight and movement—short walks count more than perfect gym plans.
  • Connection—one low-pressure social touchpoint a day (message, meal, club).
  • Reduce shame loops—if you missed a deadline, route the fix through official channels early.

HKU support (official)

CEDARS coordinates many student support services at HKU. Use their site for up-to-date programmes and contacts.
