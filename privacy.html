Home · Privacy

Privacy on a static site

This project is plain HTML, CSS, and a small amount of JavaScript. There is no login, no database, and no server-side account system in this repository.

What stays on your device

  • The Search page filters content locally. Your keystrokes are not sent to this site’s server—because there is no application server bundled here.
  • Your browser may still store normal history, cache, and downloads according to your own settings.

What happens when you leave this site

External links (for example HKU Portal or Libraries) are governed by those organisations’ own privacy statements. We open most official links in a new tab with rel="noopener noreferrer" to reduce common window.opener risks and to avoid sending referrer data in supporting browsers.

Hosting note

If you publish on GitHub Pages or a similar host, the platform may collect technical metadata (for example aggregated access logs) under its own policies. This is outside the static files in this project.

Security hygiene: keep your browser updated, use official bookmarks for sensitive tasks, and verify URLs before signing in anywhere.
